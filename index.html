Third-Party Vendor Risk Assessment Program (Enhancement)

Role: Cyber and Information Security Specialist | Duration: 1 year (2023-2024)

Project Overview: Developed and implemented a comprehensive vendor risk assessment framework to strengthen third-party risk management across the organization.

Key Activities & Controls Implemented:

  • Risk Questionnaires & Security Reviews: Created comprehensive risk assessment questionnaires and conducted detailed security reviews
  • Third-Party Criticality Classification: Identified and classified vendors based on data sensitivity, service criticality, and business dependencies
  • SLA Review & Management: Reviewed and established Service Level Agreements with clear security and performance expectations
  • Risk-Based Audit Schedule:
    • Critical third parties: Annual audits and reviews
    • Non-critical vendors: Reviews once every 3 years
  • Robust User Access Management: Implemented comprehensive access controls and monitoring for vendor access to organizational systems
  • Inventory Management: Established centralized vendor inventory with real-time tracking and documentation
  • Password Change Policy: Enforced regular password rotation and strong authentication requirements
  • Change Management Indicators: Monitoring for significant changes in service delivery or processes
  • Disruption Indicators: Early warning system for potential service disruptions or security incidents
  • Continuous Communication: Regular reviews of third-party risk management with responsible stakeholders and vendors
  • Remediation Tracking: Established systematic tracking and follow-up on identified security gaps

Frameworks & Standards: NIST CSF, ISO 27001, OSFI Guidelines B-13 & B-10

Results & Impact:

  • Successfully assessed 50+ vendors across the organization
  • Identified and documented 15 high-risk security gaps
  • Achieved 90% remediation rate within 6 months
  • Significantly improved vendor security posture and organizational risk profile
  • Enhanced compliance with regulatory requirements (OSFI B-13 & B-10)
